Core Belief


To stimulate quality, productivity and efficiency through challenging the existing methods to work collaboratively.

Our Story


  • It all initiated from risk-taking high school students unsatisfied with the apparatus they were given to work with. The impeded communication and lack of collaborative features made these, later BlueBookers, contemplate on the way to better these digital online environments.

  • At December of 2020, given the Covid outbreak, these obstacles for collaboration of online environment made it almost impossible to produce quality work with productivity, efficiency and effectiveness....We want to maximize individuals' and teams' quality, productivity and efficiency.

  • With Blue Book, anything is possible. It's flexibility creates better bonded teams that are empowered and well-managed. The user interface is user friendly while maintaining optional complexity. All in all, we challenge the existing methods of working with the one and only intention of stimulating quality, productivity and efficiency, out of you and your team.
